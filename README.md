**Hello Readers,**

Samrudhi provides service to read books in Hubli.




* Membership fee : Rs 200 per year.

* Separate cost for each book.

* Minimum 1 book per month.

* Maximum 3 books per month.

* Return policy : One month.

* Grace period is one week after return policy.

* Check for any damage before collecting the book.

### Payment
*  Cash only.


#### Contact Person : Sahana Shetty

#### Contact Number : 8073659121

#### Email ID : samrudhi.library@gmail.com

### Rules:

* Use Bookmarks.

* Do not scribble or mark on the pages.

* Do not stain the pages.

* Total cost of the book to be paid if there is any damage or if lost.

* Pay the price of book in advance.

* Refund policy - Rs 100 refundable after one year on membership cancellation.


### Address
* Om Shri Heritage Apartment, Near Aegis Auto Gas, Vidyanagar, Hubli, Karnataka ,India. Pin : 580021.

### Timings
* All days - 5 pm  to 6 pm.
*Excluding National Holidays.

5% of book fee is utilized for braille books.
